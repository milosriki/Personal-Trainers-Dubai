## Why Specialist Training Matters

General trainers often rush high-impact exercises (jumping, crunches) too soon. Our protocol is:

1.  **Reconnect:** Breath and core connection (Weeks 0-6 postpartum - cleared by doctor).
2.  **Stability:** Pelvic alignment and glute strength.
3.  **Strength:** Gradual return to lifting and conditioning.
