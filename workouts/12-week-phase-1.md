# Phase 1: Metabolic Conditioning & Foundation (Weeks 1-4) - PTD Fitness

**Goal:** Establish movement quality, improve cardiovascular health, and ignite metabolic rate.
**Frequency:** 3-4 sessions per week (non-consecutive days)
**Equipment:** Dumbbells, Resistance Bands, Mat (All provided by PTD Fitness)

## Warm-Up (10 min)

- **Mobility:** Cat-cow, thoracic rotations, hip flexor stretches
- **Activation:** Glute bridges (2x15), Band pull-aparts (2x15)
- **Pulse:** 2 min light skipping or fast walk

## The Workout (Circuit Style)

_Perform one exercise after another with minimal rest. Rest 90s after completing the full circuit. Repeat 3-4 rounds._

| Exercise                          | Reps   | Tempo  | Notes                                         |
| --------------------------------- | ------ | ------ | --------------------------------------------- |
| **1. Goblet Squat**               | 12-15  | 3-0-1  | Keep chest up, heels flat.                    |
| **2. Push-Ups (or Knees)**        | 10-12  | 2-1-1  | Core tight, elbows 45 degrees.                |
| **3. DB RDL (Romanian Deadlift)** | 12     | 3-1-1  | Hinge at hips, feel hamstring stretch.        |
| **4. Standing DB Shoulder Press** | 12     | 2-0-1  | Avoid arching lower back.                     |
| **5. DB Reverse Lunge**           | 10/leg | 2-0-1  | Step back controlled, keep front knee stable. |
| **6. Hollow Body Hold**           | 30s    | Static | Lower back pressed into floor.                |

## Cool Down (5 min)

- Static stretching: Hamstrings, Chest, Quads
- Deep breathing

---

**Why this works:** High-density training (minimal rest) creates an "afterburn" effect (EPOC), keeping your metabolism elevated for hours after the session.
